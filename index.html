🌍 Regions of Bihar & Their Specialties

Patna – Golghar, Mahavir Mandir, Bihar Museum
Gaya – Bodh Gaya, Vishnupad Temple, Buddhist Sites
Muzaffarpur – Litchi hub, Baba Garib Nath Mandir
Madhubani – Mithila paintings & folk art
Bhagalpur – Silk city, Vikramshila University
Nalanda – Ancient university ruins, Nalanda International University
Darbhanga – Raj Palace, classical music & culture
