    Interesting Facts About Bihar

    Nalanda was the world's first residential university.
    Chhath Puja is one of the oldest Hindu festivals still celebrated with devotion.
    Bihar has given India leaders like Chanakya, Ashoka, and Guru Gobind Singh.
    Madhubani paintings are a globally recognized folk art style.
